# 💕 Happy Mother's Day — Interactive Webpage

A beautiful, animated Mother's Day tribute page built with pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No frameworks, no dependencies — just open and enjoy.

---

## ✨ Features

- **🎈 Floating Balloons** — Colorful balloons drift from the bottom of the screen upward, gently swaying as they rise. New balloons continuously appear for an endless celebration effect.
- **🎆 Fireworks** — Bursts of colorful fireworks automatically light up the sky every few seconds. Click anywhere on the page to trigger your own explosions too.
- **✉️ Envelope Animation** — A pink envelope sits at the center of the page. Click it to watch the flap open and a shower of hearts burst out.
- **💌 Letter Modal** — After the envelope opens, a heartfelt handwritten letter slides in on a cream paper card with a decorative ribbon header and a close button.
- **💫 Twinkling Stars** — The deep rose night-sky background is filled with hundreds of softly pulsing stars that twinkle continuously.
- **🌸 Falling Petals** — Pink and lavender petals drift gently down the screen throughout the experience.
- **💗 Floating Hearts** — Click anywhere on the page to release little floating heart and flower emojis that float upward and fade away.

---

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Download or unzip the project folder.
2. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).
3. No internet connection required — all animations run locally.
   > *Note: Google Fonts (Playfair Display, Cormorant Garamond, Dancing Script) load from the web. If offline, the page will fall back to serif fonts.*

## 🎨 Design Details

- **Color palette:** Deep rose night sky, blush pink, soft petal tones, and gold accents
- **Typography:** Playfair Display (italic titles) · Dancing Script (letter handwriting) · Cormorant Garamond (body text)
- **Theme:** Romantic, emotional, and elegant — inspired by love letters and midnight gardens

---

## 💡 Customization Tips

- **Change the message** — Open `index.html` and find the `<p class="letter-body">` section inside the letter modal to edit the letter text.
- **Change the card message** — Find the `<div class="message-card">` section to update the paragraph shown on the main page.
- **Add a name** — Replace *"With all my love, always 💕"* in `.letter-sig` with your own name.
- **Change colors** — Edit the CSS variables at the top of the `<style>` block under `:root { }`.
